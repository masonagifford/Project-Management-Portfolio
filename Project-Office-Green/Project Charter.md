# Project Plant Pals: Operations & Training Plan

## Executive Summary:
Our plan is to provide small, low-maintenance potted plants to our top clients for their employees to have at their desks. This will be facilitated through a new application called "Plant Pals". This should raise our revenue as well as our customer satisfaction and retention.

### SMART Project Goals
- Increase revenue by 5% by rolling out a new service that provides office plants to high-volume clients.
- Increase our customer satisfaction rating to 95% within the first 3 months of rolling out the new service.
- Achieve 1,000 more unique visitors to our existing website within 9 months.

### Deliverables
- Increase brand awareness
- Send 1,000 plants to 100 customers
- Provide a catalog of office plants to high-volume clients

### Business Case / Background
**Why are we doing this?**
- This is a top-requested service by our customers and will increase revenue as well as customer satisfaction and retention.

### Benefits, Costs, and Budget
**Benefits**
- Support new service leading to 5% revenue increase, reduce late shipments and related costs, increase customer satisfaction

**Costs**
- Price of software, installation fees, time spent on hiring and training

**Budget Needed**
- $75,000

### Scope and Exclusion
**In-Scope**
- Customer service standards
- Delivery processes
- Training protocols

**Out-of-Scope**
- Product development
- Vendor contracts

### Project Team
**Project Sponsor:** Director of Operations
**Project Lead:** Project Manager
**Project Team:** Fulfillment Director, Quality Assurance Tester, Inventory Manager, Financial Analyst, Human Resources Specialist, Training Manager
**Additional Stakeholders:** VP of Customer Success, Account Manager, Receptionist, Sales Director, Sales Team, Marketing Director, Investors

### Measuring Success
**What is acceptable:**
- 5% increase in revenue by the end of the year
- 5% increase in customer satisfaction, from 90% to 95%, within the first 3 months
- Delivering 1,000 plants to 100 customers through Plant Pals
